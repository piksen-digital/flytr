Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the delay predictor?

Our delay predictor analyzes historical data, weather patterns, airline performance, and real-time conditions to achieve up to 85% accuracy for predictions made 24 hours before departure.

Is Flytr completely free to use?

Yes, the core Flytr platform is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the flight intelligence dashboard, delay predictor, and basic search functionality will always remain free.

How does Flytr make money if it's free?

Flytr operates on a affiliate model where we may receive commissions when users book through our partner links. This allows us to keep the platform free while providing valuable service.

Can I use Flytr for international travel?

Absolutely! Flytr provides comprehensive international travel intelligence including visa requirements, currency information, and country-specific travel advisories.

How often is the flight data updated?

Our flight data is updated in real-time using multiple reliable sources. Price information refreshes every 2 hours, while delay predictions are recalculated continuously as new data becomes available.
